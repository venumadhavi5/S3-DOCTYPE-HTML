famous temples in andhra pradesh

sri venkateswara swamy temple

The Sri Venkateswara Swami Temple is a Hindu temple situated in the hills of Tirumala at Tirupati in Tirupati district of Andhra Pradesh, India. The temple is dedicated to Venkateswara, a form of Vishnu, who is believed to have appeared on earth to save mankind from trials and troubles of Kali Yuga .

kanaka durga temple

King Gopinath has built the temple which is estimated to be more than 500 years old. The king dreamed the idol of Goddess Kanak Durga, and he built the temple of the goddess . As the name suggests Kanak, the idol, is completely made of Gold and is 2(two) feet in height.
