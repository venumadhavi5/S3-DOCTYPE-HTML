famous temples in tamilnadu

meenakshi amman temple

this temple was located at madhurai in tamilnadu The story of the figure of Meenakshi is also legendary. It describes a Pandya king, Malayadhvaja, who hoped for a son and heir. He carefully performed a fire ceremony requesting that the gods fulfill this wish. Instead, he was granted a daughter, Meenakshi, who was born with three breasts.

arunachaleswarar temple

Arunachalam or "Annamalai" is located in the state of Tamil Nadu. Arunachalam is one of the Panchabhutalinga Kshetras. Agnibhuta is symbolized in Panchalinga Kshetras in South India. Arunachalam means Aruna - red, Achalam - hill. Meaning red hill. A-runa means that which removes sins. It is called "Thiruvannamalai" in Tamil.
