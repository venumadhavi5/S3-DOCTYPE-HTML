chilkur balaji temple

the temple was built by a devotee who could not visit the famous Tirupati Temple due to his health condition

ramappa temple

The medieval Deccan Ramappa Temple which dates back to 1213 AD, was built by the patronage of the Kakatiya ruler Kakati Ganapathi Deva under the authority of his Chief Commander Rudra Samani at the place known as Ranakude in the Atukuru province .

birla mandir

The first Birla Mandir was built in Delhi in 1939, collectively by Ghanshyamdas Birla and his brothers, as well as his father .
